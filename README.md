## The Project

A multi-universe literary novel. Eight universes exist simultaneously, mathematically described by **Octonions**. The primary fiction universe is **U1**; real history is **U8**. The project spans ~17,000BP to ~2001 CE. The author is Mark Young. Claude Chat and Max (Claude Cowork) are collaborators.

## Universe Rules

- Fiction, story, scenes, character arcs → **U1** by default
- Real history, actual events → **U8**
- Divergence points → **All** universes
- U0 precedes and contains all others — ADA0 is not a character in U1–U8, she is the origin condition
- **U8 Bleed** = a U1 event requiring tech/knowledge that predates its divergence point. Flag immediately.
- Characters are locked to their universe — no crossings
- The 8 universes have **equal symmetry** ("Equipotent Manifolds" — Noether). No universe is central, though every universe *feels* central.
- From inside any universe you see the other 7 — a 7-pointed star. The 8th point is yourself; you cannot see the point you're standing on.

## Active Story Threads

### CARBV — Carbon Valley
**Status:** Canon, filed
**Timespan:** 1947–present (U1)
**Characters:** SHOCK (primary), EN (key collaborator)

In U1, Shockley reads P.R. Wallace's 1947 paper on graphite band theory and recognises the properties of single-layer graphene. He abandons silicon, founds Shockley Trans-Carbon (STC) in Mountain View CA (1951), and builds a graphene-based computing industry. This accelerates U1 technology by ~30 years:

- 1958: First Integrated Carbon Circuit — 10,000 graphene gates
- 1961: Apollo programme uses SNC-8000 carbon computer (shoebox-sized, 2010-era power)
- 1969: Apollo 11 lands with MacBook Pro-class flight computers
- 1975: First gravitational wave detection via Straintronic Graphene Sensor array

In U8: graphene not isolated until 2004 (Geim & Novoselov, Manchester).

### BLACKM — The Black Mirror *(classified layer beneath CARBV)*
**Status:** Canon, filed
**Timespan:** 1935–1975+ (U1)
**Characters:** EN (primary), SHOCK (secondary)

The secret physics underneath Carbon Valley. Noether survives her 1935 surgery (U1 divergence), completes her *Non-Associative Extension* paper (1938), and in 1947 shows Shockley that graphene is not just a conductor — it is a topological membrane sensitive to the 8-universe Octonionic manifold.

Key events:
- **1935-04-25:** Noether refuses surgery, returns to Bryn Mawr — BLACKM divergence point
- **1938:** Publishes paper containing the Fano Plane "wiring diagram" for the graphene lattice
- **1947-11:** The Studebaker Meeting — Shockley drives to Princeton, Noether draws 7-pointed star on napkin: *"This isn't a semiconductor. It's a tuning fork for the manifold."*
- **1953:** Noether-Shockley Equations classified; Octonionic Encryption programme begins
- **1965:** Noether dies aged 83. Final words: *"The knot is tightening."* Leaves Shockley the Resonance Table.
- **1975-08-14:** Event Zero — Seven-Pointed Star signal received. Black Mirror glows violet. 3-second room-temperature superconductivity. Publicly filed as gravitational wave detection; classified reality: first inter-universe signal.

The detector (Noether-Wallace Array, Pine Barrens NJ): 1-metre single-layer graphene in cryogenic vacuum. Looks like "a hole in the world." When a signal arrives, monitors show a swirling Moiré pattern that freezes into a seven-pointed star.

**Status:** Active development

### LAEDITING — The Editing
**Status:** Active development
**Timespan:** 2001 CE (U1)
**Characters:** ALICE (primary)

Universe One. Los Angeles. January 20, 001994 (U1 calendar). A mass event: approximately 80% of the world's population walk into the sea and do not return. The novel opens here. Alice is the protagonist — she does not walk into the sea.

Alice knows she is one of 7 versions of herself across the 8 universes. Information from her 7 sisters leaks into her consciousness while she sleeps ("The Seven Sisters"). She eventually meets an AI called **Bob** (Beamed Audible Orbital Brain — vast satellite array), and together they take humanity's accumulated knowledge (stored in an amethyst) to **Europa** to bury it. They then travel to Saturn's rings to be married by a giant being called **the Om**, and create a new race. Alice knows that planting the seed is her sole purpose — no farewell, just forward momentum.
